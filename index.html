Spyglass

Spyglass is a very fast Clojure client for Memcached built on top of Spy Memcached

Get Started! » Join the Mailing List » Contribute »

Very fast

Spy Memcached is battle tested and highly optimized. Spyglass has very low overhead on top of that.

Feature complete

Spyglass supports almost all of the Memcached protocol operations as well as both binary and text protocols.

Asynchronous or synchronous

Spyglass supports synchronous and asynchronous implementations of protocol operations.

Not just Memcached

Spyglass can also be used with Membase Server and the Kestrel message queue and other servers that use the Memcached protocol.
